Executive Producer — Live Events
Event Production Full-Time Travel ~5–7 days/mo Senior

The Executive Producer (in-person, live events) owns pre-show planning and the implementation of production services to ensure a successful client experience. You’ll lead a team of creative and technical thinkers while managing client expectations through the planning, onsite, and post-event phases — working closely with our Account, Creative, Technical, Digital Services, and Operations teams to deliver flawless, on-budget programs including in-person, hybrid events, and studio broadcasts.

Successful candidates will

  • Manage client communication and the production planning process for an assigned project, including the development of necessary assets (production timelines, show flow and scripts, room diagrams, project deliverables, etc.)
  • Manage the implementation and/or onsite execution of all elements for the project
  • Manage the post-project follow-up and reporting process
  • Be a strategic thinker
  • Deliver exceptional customer service in a fast-paced, client-focused environment

Additional qualifications

  • This is not a meeting-planning position
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Media/Video Production, Stage/Event Management, Theatre/Technical Direction, or equivalent work experience
  • Seven to ten years in a production setting — agency, event/broadcast, or AV environment
  • Strong team management skills; highly organized with the ability to multi-task
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
  • Proficiency in office software (Microsoft Office, Apple iWork, etc.)
  • Knowledge of Adobe, AutoCAD/Vectorworks, and similar design applications a plus
  • Travel required — approximately 5–7 days a month

Live Event Technical Director / Project Manager
Technical Direction Chicago Full-Time Senior

We’re looking for a Technical Director/Project Manager to coordinate the technical and production elements of live, virtual, and hybrid corporate meetings and events — ensuring the right technology solutions, suppliers, and resources are allocated for flawless execution. The ideal candidate has a strong background in live-event technical direction and a genuine desire to ideate and contribute to the look, feel, and design of the project. This is a senior-level position with salary commensurate with experience.

Essential job functions

  • Manage all aspects of in-person and hybrid event production
  • Provide expert knowledge and recommendations to support project objectives, design, technological solutions, and quality standards
  • Manage or attend client input and planning sessions; maintain dialogue with stakeholders to support ongoing project needs
  • Manage internal teams, create the project plan, and facilitate project and team processes
  • Conduct site inspections of venues; research local vendor and labor support
  • Create show flows, manage rehearsals, and call show when needed
  • Acquire presentation content, run tech rehearsals, and prepare for live delivery
  • Troubleshoot with the technical team to find solutions for technical issues
  • Create and share post-production reports and feedback
  • Attend industry events to learn new methodologies and technologies and recommend/incorporate them into future events
  • Manage multiple projects/events with many variables, tight deadlines, and conflicting priorities
  • Build positive relationships with high-level clients and provide first-class support

Key skills & talents

  • Technical direction and show-calling experience
  • Creative mind capable of generating or interpreting concept design ideas
  • Strong knowledge of event technologies
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office; proficient in Vectorworks, AutoCAD, or similar room-layout software
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • High level of accuracy and attention to detail
  • Decisive, successful problem-solving skills; results through teamwork and collaboration
  • Produces results in a fast-paced, high-pressure, deadline-driven environment
  • Ability to think strategically, understand business needs, and apply creative solutions
  • Travel will be required

Account Executive — Live Event & AV Sales
Business Development US-Based Full-Time Salary + Commission

LSAV POWERHOUSE is looking for a Live Event & Audio-Visual Industry Account Executive to generate company sales of event production elements — AV technology, event production, creative, and custom scenic services — for live, virtual, and hybrid corporate meetings and events. The ideal candidate has a strong background in live-event industry sales, an active client base, and a genuine desire to ideate and contribute to the overall success of each project. This is a senior-level position with salary and commission commensurate with experience.

Who you are

  • Passionate about business development, cultivating sales relationships, and searching for new business
  • Successful selling audiovisual and production support services
  • Have an active client base
  • Comfortable communicating with all levels and functions of an organization, particularly in the live-events industry
  • Self-motivated with a competitive attitude

You’ll be responsible for

  • Generating new prospects and following up on warm leads
  • Networking at industry meetings and conferences
  • Ensuring the highest level of client satisfaction, overseeing all events from inception to post-show measurement
  • Managing the full sales process from prospecting to established business
  • Presenting full event services: audio, video, scenic, lighting, technical direction, stage management, creative options, transportation, and venue logistics
  • Creating timely sales reports and demonstrating accountability for monthly sales goals and performance

Desired skills & experience

  • 5+ years of sales experience in corporate meetings, trade shows, event management, or a similar industry
  • Ability to develop and manage RFP and proposal processes, tight project timelines, vendors, and budgets for complex large-scale events
  • Proven track record of pipeline management and closing sales
  • Selling event staging or production, with a clear understanding of video, audio, lighting, rigging, scenic, and AV technology and their applications
  • A book of business that can be readily converted to sales within the first 12 months
  • Proficiency in Word, Excel, and CRM software

Who you are
